Passage 1

In a small village in southern France, the local market is held every Saturday. Farmers bring fresh vegetables and fruits, while artisans sell handcrafted items. The market has been a gathering place for the community for over a century.

  1. What is the main purpose of the passage?
  2. According to the passage, which of the following is NOT sold at the market?

Passage 2

Global warming is caused by the buildup of greenhouse gases, which trap heat in the Earth's atmosphere. If left unchecked, it could lead to rising sea levels and more extreme weather events.

  1. What is the main effect of greenhouse gases mentioned in the passage?
  2. Which consequence is predicted if global warming continues?
